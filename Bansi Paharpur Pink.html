Bansi Paharpur Pink — Classical Expression in Natural Stone

Material Identity

Bansi Paharpur Pink is distinguished by its fine grain structure and soft blush tone, making it highly suitable for classical and ornamental architecture. Its natural warmth enhances arches, carved facades, and heritage-inspired structures where refined detailing is essential.

The stone’s workability allows intricate carving, profile shaping, and architectural detailing, making it a preferred material for temples, palatial villas, and luxury residential developments.
