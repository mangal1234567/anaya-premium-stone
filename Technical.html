Sandstone Technical Specifications & Performance

Performance characteristics and dimensional standards relevant for architectural specification and international supply.

Material Performance Characteristics

• Suitable compressive strength for façade and structural wall applications

• Moderate water absorption typical of architectural-grade sandstone

• Good flexural performance for cladding systems

• Density range appropriate for exterior and landscape installations

• Weather-resistant performance under standard environmental exposure

Dimensional & Processing Capability

• Precision cutting using gang saw and calibrated machinery

• Slabs, tiles, and cut-to-size panel production

• Custom thickness and dimensional tolerance management

• Surface finishing: Natural, Honed, Sandblasted, Machine Cut

• Block supply for carving and architectural detailing

Testing & Certification

Laboratory testing can be conducted in accordance with applicable Indian and international standards. Detailed test certificates and performance reports are available upon request for project-specific supply.

Natural Material Variation

As a natural stone, sandstone may exhibit inherent variation in color, grain structure, and physical characteristics. Technical values may vary within acceptable industry tolerances depending on quarry bench and batch.
