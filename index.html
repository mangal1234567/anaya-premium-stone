About ANAY Premium Stone

ANAY Premium Stone is a Rajasthan-based manufacturer and exporter of premium architectural sandstone including Dholpur Beige, Dholpur Red, and Bansi Paharpur Pink. Located in the sandstone region of Sarmathura, Dholpur, we source stone directly from regional quarries and process it in-house to ensure consistent quality and precision.

Our sandstone products are widely used for facade cladding, paving, landscaping, and architectural projects worldwide. Through calibrated stone processing and reliable export logistics, we supply natural sandstone solutions to architects, builders, and stone distributors across the USA, UK, Europe, UAE, and Australia.

Architectural Applications

Facade Cladding

Natural sandstone is widely used for architectural facade cladding, adding durability and timeless beauty to building exteriors. Dholpur Beige, Dholpur Red, and Bansi Paharpur Pink sandstone provide a strong, weather-resistant surface suitable for residential, commercial, and architectural elevations.

Landscape Paving

Natural sandstone paving is ideal for gardens, walkways, patios, and public plazas due to its strength and natural slip resistance. Dholpur Beige and Dholpur Red sandstone provide durable and elegant paving solutions for landscape and outdoor architectural spaces.

Exterior Flooring

Natural sandstone is ideal for courtyards, terraces, and outdoor spaces because of its strength and natural slip resistance. Dholpur Beige and Dholpur Red sandstone provide durable and elegant flooring for residential and architectural exterior areas.

Pool Decking

Natural sandstone is an excellent choice for pool decks because it stays relatively cool under sunlight and provides good slip resistance. Dholpur Beige and Dholpur Red sandstone create durable, comfortable, and elegant surfaces around swimming pools.

Garden Pathways

Natural sandstone paving stones are ideal for garden pathways and landscape walkways, offering durability and a timeless natural look. Dholpur Beige and Dholpur Red sandstone create elegant paths that blend beautifully with outdoor landscapes.

Wall Cladding

Natural sandstone wall cladding adds strength, texture, and timeless character to exterior walls. Dholpur Beige, Dholpur Red, and Bansi Paharpur Pink sandstone are widely used for durable and elegant architectural wall surfaces.
