Dholpur Red — A Stone of Structural Authority

Material Identity

Dholpur Red is defined by its deep natural coloration and commanding architectural presence. Sourced from the Dholpur sandstone belt, this material has long been specified for governmental, institutional, and large-scale projects where durability and permanence are essential.

Its structural density and weather resistance make it suitable for monumental façades, structural walls, and high-load applications requiring long-term performance.
