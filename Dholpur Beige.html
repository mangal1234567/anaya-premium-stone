Dholpur Beige Sandstone

Material Overview

Dholpur Beige is a warm-toned architectural sandstone sourced from the Dholpur belt of Rajasthan. Recognized for its uniform grain and structural stability, it is widely specified for façades, paving systems, institutional developments, and large-scale architectural projects.
