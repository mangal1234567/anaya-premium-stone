Premium Rajasthan Sandstone for Global Architectural Projects

Rooted in Source. Built for Export.

ANAY Premium Stone is a Rajasthan-based sandstone manufacturer and exporter located in Sarmathura, Dholpur — one of India’s most recognized sandstone belts. We specialize in high-quality Dholpur Beige, Dholpur Red, and Bansi Paharpur Pink sandstone for global architectural and landscaping projects.

With direct access to controlled quarry sources, we ensure long-term raw material stability, color consistency, and dependable bulk supply. From block selection to final finishing, every stage is managed within our integrated in-house processing facility.

Our sandstone slabs, tiles, and custom architectural components are supplied to architects, contractors, and stone importers across the USA, UK, Europe, UAE, and Australia.

Structured Manufacturing & Export Discipline

Our manufacturing infrastructure includes calibrated gang saw machines, precision cutting lines, and finishing systems designed for consistent production output.

Production workflows follow strict export standards including dimensional tolerance checks, shade consistency evaluation, and secure palletized container packaging for international shipments.
