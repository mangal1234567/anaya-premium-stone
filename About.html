From Sarmathura’s Quarries to Global Architectural Projects

Rooted in Source. Built for Export.

ANAY Premium Stone is based in Sarmathura, Dholpur, a famous sandstone region in India. With direct access to an extensive quarry network, we carefully select high-quality stone blocks to ensure consistent texture, superior strength, and reliable long-term supply. This allows us to maintain strict quality control from quarry to final product.

Every block is processed in our in-house facility using calibrated gang saws and precision cutting systems, allowing dimensional accuracy for slabs, tiles, and project-specific architectural requirements.

Structured Manufacturing & Export Discipline

Our production workflow is designed specifically for international buyers — focusing on consistent sizing, finish uniformity, and project-based customization. From surface finishing to packaging, each stage follows controlled inspection protocols.

We prepare material for containerized exports with moisture protection, pallet optimization, and documentation compliance suitable for USA, UK, Europe, UAE, and Australian markets.

Our Operational Philosophy

Quarry Control

Direct sourcing from controlled sandstone belts of Dholpur ensures material consistency across bulk volumes.

Precision Processing

Dimensional tolerance management for slabs, tiles, cladding panels, and custom-cut architectural elements.

Global Supply Approach

Structured export workflow designed for architects, contractors, and stone importers seeking reliability.

Stone Solutions for Timeless Spaces

We collaborate with architects, developers, and distributors who prioritize material authenticity, structural reliability, and refined architectural expression.
