Material Applications

Representative architectural applications demonstrating the versatility, structural reliability, and aesthetic character of our sandstone materials.

Exterior Wall Cladding

Dholpur Beige and Dholpur Red are widely specified for exterior wall systems, offering dimensional precision, structural reliability, and long-term weather resistance in residential and institutional developments.

Landscape & Paving Systems

Sandstone paving solutions are suitable for pathways, courtyards, public plazas, and private landscapes, combining durability with natural slip resistance and architectural harmony.

Columns, Arches & Architectural Detailing

Bansi Paharpur Pink sandstone is particularly suited for classical architectural elements including carved columns, arches, cornices, and ornamental detailing in heritage and contemporary projects.

Institutional & Structural Facades

Dholpur Red sandstone is commonly used in large-scale institutional buildings and monumental facades, delivering strong visual character combined with long-term structural performance.
