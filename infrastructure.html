Sandstone Manufacturer in Dholpur, Rajasthan

ANAY Premium Stone operates an integrated sandstone quarry and manufacturing unit in Dholpur, Rajasthan, delivering architectural-grade Indian sandstone to international construction and landscaping projects.

Integrated Sandstone Quarry – Dholpur Rajasthan

Located in the Dholpur sandstone belt of Rajasthan, our quarry access ensures consistent block availability, natural color uniformity, structural durability, and long-term supply stability. Every sandstone block is carefully inspected before entering our manufacturing process.

Sandstone Manufacturing Unit & Gang Saw Processing

Our sandstone manufacturing unit in Dholpur is equipped with gang saw cutting machines, calibration systems, and finishing equipment to produce slabs, tiles, paving stones, wall cladding, and custom architectural stone formats with dimensional precision.

Quality Inspection & Production Control

Each production batch undergoes dimensional verification, surface inspection, and thickness tolerance checks before packaging. Technical performance data including compressive strength, density, and water absorption can be shared upon request.

Export Packaging & International Dispatch

As a sandstone exporter from Rajasthan, India, materials are packed in fumigated wooden crates with moisture protection and reinforced pallet alignment for secure containerized shipment to USA, UK, UAE, Europe, and Australia.
