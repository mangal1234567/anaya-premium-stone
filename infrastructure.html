Quarry Control & Manufacturing Infrastructure

Integrated sourcing, calibrated processing, and structured export handling for architectural-grade sandstone supply.

Controlled Quarry Access

Our operations are based in Sarmathura, Dholpur — a recognized sandstone belt. With access to family-leased quarry networks, block selection is conducted with focus on grain consistency, structural integrity, and long-term supply stability.

Processing Facility

In-house gang saw cutting systems and calibrated machinery allow dimensional precision for slabs, tiles, and custom architectural formats. Production workflows are structured to support both volume-based supply and project-specific requirements.

Quality Control & Inspection

Each batch undergoes dimensional verification and surface inspection before packing. Finish consistency, thickness tolerance, and pallet alignment are monitored to ensure readiness for containerized export.

Export Packaging & Dispatch

Materials are packed in fumigated wooden crates with protective wrapping, corner guards, and moisture barriers. Container loading is optimized for safe international transit and documentation compliance.
