Dholpur Beige Sandstone

Material Overview

Dholpur Beige is a warm-toned architectural sandstone sourced from the sandstone belts of Dholpur, Rajasthan. Known for its uniform texture and structural stability, it is widely specified for contemporary façades, cladding systems, exterior paving, and landscape developments.

Applications

• Exterior wall cladding

• Façade panels

• Landscape paving

• Boundary walls

• Staircases & steps

• Institutional projects

Available Finishes

• Natural

• Honed

• Sandblasted

• Machine Cut

Available Formats

Slabs, tiles, cut-to-size panels, and custom architectural elements manufactured with dimensional tolerance control for export markets.
